# HyperBox Beta Testers Hall of Fame

## Recognition Program
This document celebrates the contributions of our beta testers who help shape HyperBox and drive continuous improvement.

## Week 1 (Feb 19-25, 2026)

### Pending Confirmations

## Recognition Criteria
- Bug reports and detailed reproduction steps
- Feature suggestions with use cases
- Performance testing and benchmarks
- Documentation improvements
- Community engagement and referrals

## How to Contribute
To be featured in our Hall of Fame:
1. Test HyperBox on your projects
2. Report bugs with clear details
3. Suggest features you'd like to see
4. Share your experience in GitHub Discussions
5. Help others troubleshoot issues

Thank you for helping make HyperBox better!
